About The Mkurugenzi

The journey of a storyteller who became a voice for a generation

My Story & Vision

Born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya, I discovered my passion for storytelling at a young age. Growing up in a vibrant community filled with diverse characters and experiences, I learned that everyone has a story worth telling.

My journey began in radio, where I learned the power of voice and narrative. This foundation taught me how to connect with audiences intimately, a skill that would later define my filmmaking style.

The breakthrough came with "Real Househelps of Kawangware" - a series that dared to tell stories from perspectives often overlooked in mainstream media. This success taught me that audiences crave authenticity - stories that reflect their own experiences, culture, and society.

Creative Philosophy

I believe in storytelling that:

  • Reflects authentic African experiences
  • Celebrates our cultural diversity
  • Challenges societal norms
  • Inspires positive change
  • Entertains while educating

My Journey Timeline

2008-2010

Radio Beginnings

Started as a radio presenter at local stations, developing characters and narratives that resonated with urban Kenyan audiences. Learned the art of audio storytelling.

2011-2013

Scriptwriting Development

Transitioned to television scriptwriting, working on popular Kenyan shows. Developed my signature style of blending comedy with social commentary.

2014-2016

Breakthrough with RHOK

Created and wrote for "Real Househelps of Kawangware," a series that became a cultural phenomenon and won multiple awards including Kalasha Awards.

2017-2019

Film Transition & Expansion

Transitioned to film with "Click Click Bang" and other productions. Started my YouTube channel "Mkurugenzi" to connect directly with audiences.

2020-Present

Digital Empire & Mentorship

Built a digital media empire with podcast, vlogs, and online community. Started mentoring young filmmakers through workshops and masterclasses.
